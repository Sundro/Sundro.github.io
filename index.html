THE GAME

Play with your friends (or with Companions Particularly Unintelligent if you don’t have any) and challenge your friendship in hilarious no-hold barred matches to show who is the real “Jump Master”! Your objective is clear: the more heads you jump on, the more points you gets!

Your body weight isn’t your only weapon to fight your opponents; you can use a ridiculous but deadly arsenal to unleash your supremacy: wear a SM suit to win points when people jump on you, use your underpants to blind your rivals, recreate the “You Shall not Pass!” scene with a shockwave fart, summon Nyancats, cheese slices and twitter birds falling from the sky and more!

Participate in crazy game modes and add a new twist to your brawls: be a selfie addict and win point by jumping in the frame each time a picture is taken, avoid a rain of explosive dog poop or brawl in a place where time is completely broken!
